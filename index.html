ABOUT IUA

"International University Ala-Too"

"Your window to the world"

The Ministry of Education and Science of the Kyrgyz Republic is the State governing body. The founder of the International University “Ala-Too” is the International Educational Institution “Sapat”. Ala-Too International University (AMU) was established in 1996, which is located in the city of Bishkek, Kyrgyz Republic. MUA is a legal entity and carries out its activities in accordance with the legislation of the Kyrgyz Republic. The governing bodies of the university are: - supreme body - Founder; - executive body - Rector - Sanzharbek Erdolatov; - collegial body - the academic council of the university.

Our university consists of five blocks, where there are classrooms and laboratories equipped with the Internet and modern interactive whiteboards. Our “Town” is an educational center with a library, conference hall, 3 stadiums, 1 tennis court, 1 gym, 6 computer classes, 1 Internet club, 1 research center, 2 cantinas, 1 medical center.

The AMU has 4 faculties, 3 institutes and 16 departments. The main language of instruction is English. Russian, Kyrgyz and Turkish are auxiliary languages. In addition, students of individual faculties can choose a second foreign language; their choice is Japanese, German, Chinese, and French. For those who do not speak English, there are preparatory courses. Upon graduation from the university, a state diploma of higher professional education of the Kyrgyz Republic is issued.

The university has students from more than 20 countries. In order to strengthen its position in the educational services market, the university is working to open new educational programs that are in demand in the new information-competitive conditions.

For the period 1996-2017. There have been changes in the structure of the university - in the expansion and introduction of new educational technologies. The staff of the AMU consists of highly qualified, experienced, young, active teachers from Kyrgyzstan, Turkey, the USA, China, India and other countries. Bachelor's, Master's and postgraduate programs are conducted in a multicultural and democratic atmosphere of mutual respect. Our university has bilateral agreements with some universities in Europe, China, America, Turkey and Cyprus.

We exchange students and teaching staff under the following programs: Mevlana, Erasmus. Such programs provide an opportunity for students and teachers to continue their studies for 1-2 semesters at various universities and share their experience.

Having received a highly professional education, computer knowledge, confidence and life experience, having mastered foreign languages ​​and etiquette, 80% of our university graduates easily enter into a modern, information-driven, competitive working life.
